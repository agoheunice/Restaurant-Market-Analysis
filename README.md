## Introduction:

This project analyzes restaurant ratings and consumer preferences to identify the factors that influence restaurant performance and customer satisfaction. 
Five separate datasets were cleaned, transformed, and merged into a single master dataset using Microsoft Excel Power Query. The consolidated data was then analyzed using PivotTables, PivotCharts, and an interactive Excel dashboard to generate insights that support restaurant owners and potential investors in making data-driven business decisions.

### Analysis:

The project began by cleaning and integrating five related datasets containing restaurant information, customer ratings, consumer demographics, and cuisine preferences. Using Power Query, the datasets were standardized and merged into a single master table, providing a comprehensive dataset for analysis.

The analysis explored restaurant performance, customer preferences, and demographic trends. Results identified the highest-rated restaurants, the cuisines most preferred by consumers, and the characteristics associated with higher restaurant ratings, including pricing, franchise status, and available services. Consumer demographic analysis also highlighted the dominant customer segments, providing context for interpreting restaurant preferences and market demand.

An interactive dashboard was developed to enable users to filter results dynamically and compare restaurant performance across different business characteristics, supporting evidence-based decision-making for restaurant management and investment opportunities.

## Conclusion and Recommendations

The analysis demonstrates that restaurant performance is influenced by a combination of customer preferences, restaurant characteristics, and service offerings. Restaurant owners should focus on maintaining high-quality service while aligning their menu offerings with customer demand. Investors can use the identified performance indicators, such as restaurant ratings, cuisine popularity, and operational characteristics, to evaluate potential investment opportunities more effectively.

The interactive dashboard provides an efficient decision-support tool for exploring restaurant performance, customer preferences, and market trends from multiple perspectives.
